OUR APPROACH

Rankings Requires a Comprehensive SEO Strategy

Technical SEO Audit

If your site is not technically sound, it doesn't matter how good your content or offer is, it's very hard to have high rankings. We conduct a thorough deep dive of your site, reviewing site architecture, load times, xml sitemap, robots.txt, crawl errors, click depth, rich snippets, url structure, headings, meta tags, canonical urls, duplicate content, internal links, and more. These audits are typically 10-50 pages in length depending on site size.

Keyword Research

Ranking on the first page is about being intentional. You can accelerate the ranking process by identifying keywords with high search volume, less organic competition, and a high cost-per-click or high commercial intent. Understanding and implementing semantically related keywords within your content is critical for creating content that Google (and potential customers) deem valuable. Intention, targeting, content, and budget to achieve your goals are all informed by keyword research.

On-Site SEO & Content

Content is a critical piece of SEO strategy. Just writing a 1500 word blog post isn't enough to rank in competitive markets. Google's quality evaluator guidelines use the acronym E-A-T to determine what every high-quality page needs: a high level of Expertise, Authority, and Trustworthiness. Like all things SEO, this take time to build, but is worth it. Building a high E-A-T is a long term play and makes it very difficult to lose your rankings.

Offsite SEO & Link Building

Based on insights from our data gathering process, our team will create multiple hypothesis to improve your sites conversion rate. Potential tests are prioritized via a model that scores the test by impact and ease of implementation. For example, tests that are above the fold are noticed by more people and are thus more likely to have an impact.

Adding non-brand organic traffic to your acquisition channel mix can have a tremendous impact on your business. Ranking in position #1 generates an exponentially higher CTR than the bottom half of the page. Every business that I've met that is in it for the long-term regrets not starting SEO sooner.
