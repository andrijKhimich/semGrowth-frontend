What is Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)?

Conversion Rate Optimization is the art and science of turning website visitors into customers via maximizing the effectiveness of your website. Even deeper, it's the process of identifying what experiences and messages most resonate with your visitors. Lessons learned from CRO can be applied across all marketing activities to maximize your funnel, increase marketshare, and make more money.

OUR APPROACH

Stay Ahead of Your Competition with a Customized Optimization Plan

Analytics Insights & Health Check

Prior to launching any tests we check to ensure your analytics and goal tracking are functioning properly. Second, we dive into your existing data to map your funnel, understand your customer, and uncover potential quick wins.

Proven Ideas

We've launched thousands of tests across multiple verticals, which means we have a vault of winning test ideas that have already proven to improve the percentage of visitors that convert.

Heatmaps, Funnels, Recordings

We add to your site: heatmaps (click, move, scroll), funnel tracking (where are your visitors dropping off?), and video recordings of your visitors to eliminate guesswork and gain a better understanding of their behavior on your site.

Prioritization Framework & Roadmap

Based on insights from our data gathering process, our team will create multiple hypothesis to improve your sites conversion rate. Potential tests are prioritized via a model that scores the test by impact and ease of implementation. For example, tests that are above the fold are noticed by more people and are thus more likely to have an impact.
