about the founder

Jesse is a marketer, entrepreuner, and angel investor who has spent the last 15 years using customer acquisition strategies to build great companies.

He co-founded, scaled, and ultimately sold a D2C brand that today sits on the shelves of major retailers, including Albertsons and CVS. Jesse is a former accountant with executive education from MIT. He was in 500 Startups Batch 12 in San Francisco, where he ran growth marketing for a marketplace startup that he helped scale to 130 countries and raised over $10 million from major funds. He started SEM Growth to partner with select businesses to help them grow via optimizing their paid and organic marketing channels.

I do not run a sales-driven “churn & burn” agency where an outsourced junior practioner manages your campaigns.“

Jesse Leonardi / Founder & CEO
